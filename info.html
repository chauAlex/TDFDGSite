Movement

All camera movement is done using WASD and your mouse scroll wheel. The scroll wheel will adjust the height of the camera.

Tower Placement

If you have enough money to purchase a tower, you can click on its card on the top left and click whichever placement pod is available.

Upgrades/Sell

Upgrades can increase your firerate, firepower, and even add special abilities. To upgrade or sell a turret, click on the body of the turret to pop up a UI for these actions.

Music Stoppage

Music Stoppage is a powerful skill that can only be used for a total of 20 seconds while active. When the music is paused using the disc on the left, the enemies lose motivation to keep advancing, but instead begin targeting your turrets. This action buys you time to kill the enemies, but also risks your turrets corroding to the slimes.

Speakers

The speakers will charge on their own as long as the music keeps going, and will light up green when they are ready to blast. Clicking on the speaker body will emit a shrill of sound that will severely damage and slow enemies in its path.
